Description Flipkart is one of India's leading e-commerce companies, known for revolutionizing the online shopping experience in the country. Founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, Flipkart started as an online bookstore and quickly expanded its product range to include electronics, fashion, home goods, and much more. Headquartered in Bangalore, Flipkart has grown to become a significant player in the Indian retail market, offering millions of products across various categories. The platform is known for its user-friendly interface, wide product selection, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery services. Flipkart has also introduced several innovative features such as cash on delivery, no-cost EMI options, and easy returns to cater to the diverse needs of Indian consumers. In 2018, Walmart acquired a majority stake in Flipkart, further boosting its market presence and operational capabilities. Today, Flipkart continues to be a trusted and popular choice for online shopping, serving millions of customers across India with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Services offered by flipkart are:-

  • Online Shopping: Flipkart provides an extensive online shopping platform where customers can purchase a wide variety of products, including electronics, fashion, home and kitchen items, books, and more.
  • Flipkart Plus: A membership program that offers benefits such as free and faster shipping, early access to sales, and exclusive offers.
  • Electronics and Gadgets: Flipkart offers a comprehensive selection of electronic products, including smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, and home appliances.
  • Fashion and Lifestyle: The platform offers a vast array of clothing, footwear, accessories, and beauty products for men, women, and children.
  • Flipkart Wholesale: A platform for retailers to buy products in bulk at wholesale prices.
More facts about Flipkart!
  • In 2018, Walmart acquired a 77% stake in Flipkart for $16 billion, making it one of the largest e-commerce acquisitions in the world.
  • Flipkart acquired Myntra, a major online fashion retailer, in 2014 to strengthen its position in the fashion segment. This acquisition helped Flipkart become a leader in the online fashion market in India.
